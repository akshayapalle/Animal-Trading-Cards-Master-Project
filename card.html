Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, known for their intelligence and strong family bonds.

  • Scientific Name: Loxodonta
  • Average Height: 3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 60-70 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, forests, and savannas

Elephants are herbivorous mammals characterized by their long trunks, tusks, large ears, and thick skin. They are found in Africa and Asia, and they play an important role in their ecosystems as keystone species.
